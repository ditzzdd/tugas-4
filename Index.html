One Stage. Thousands of Stories

In a fast-paced world, music is the universal language that unites souls. FuturaFestt is more than just an event - it's a celebration of freedom, passion, and boundless energy. Lineup Artis FuturaFertt

Get ready for an unforgettable musical experience! FuturaFertt features a lineup of world-renowned artists - from melodious voices to pounding beats. Feel the incredible energy of our grand stage.

Adele

Singer

International singer known for her unique voice and hits like "Hello" and "Someone Like You.".

Billie Eilish

Singer

American singer and songwriter known for her unique and expressive musical style, with hits such as “Bad Guy” and “Happier Than Ever”.

Bruno Mars

Singer

Bruno Mars is a singer, songwriter, and producer known for his unique style of funk, pop, and soul music. His charismatic act and hits like “Just the Way You Are” and “Uptown Funk”

Singer
